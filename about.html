About UGANE Business Services

Excellence, Innovation, and Trust Since Inception

Who We Are

UGANE Business Services is a diversified conglomerate operating across eight major business sectors. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional quality, innovative solutions, and unmatched customer service across all our divisions.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a leader in the Nigerian business landscape, with operations extending across multiple continents.

Our Mission

To provide world-class products and services across multiple industries while maintaining the highest standards of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We aim to be the bridge between international excellence and local needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading diversified business services provider in Nigeria and beyond, recognized for excellence, innovation, and positive impact on communities. We envision a future where UGANE is synonymous with trust and quality.
