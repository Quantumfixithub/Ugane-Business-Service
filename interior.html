Transforming Spaces, Creating Experiences

UGANE Interior Division offers full-service interior design and decoration, from concept development and space planning to procurement and project management. Our team works with clients to deliver tailored interiors that reflect brand identity, lifestyle, and functionality.

We handle residential, commercial, and hospitality projects — coordinating with trusted suppliers and craftsmen to ensure quality finishes and timely delivery.

UGANE's interior design services include residential interior design, commercial fit-outs, hospitality design, bespoke furniture design, and full project management. Our design approach balances aesthetics, functionality and value — helping clients achieve beautiful interiors that perform well in everyday use.
