Trusted Household Brands & Reliable Support

UGANE sources household equipment ranging from kitchen appliances, cleaning equipment, small electrics, to home comfort products. We balance performance, warranty coverage and cost-effectiveness so you get the best value.

Our procurement team vets suppliers for authenticity and quality, ensuring every product we deliver meets safety standards and expected durability for both residential and hospitality use.

Our household equipment categories include: kitchen appliances (cookers, ovens, hobs), refrigeration (fridges, freezers), laundry equipment (washers, dryers), small appliances (blenders, coffee machines), and cleaning equipment (vacuums, steam cleaners). We support bulk orders and corporate supply agreements.
