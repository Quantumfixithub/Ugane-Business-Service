FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our food export services

What products do you export?

We export a wide range of agricultural products including cashew nuts, cocoa beans, ginger, sesame seeds, hibiscus flowers, shea butter, and various other Nigerian agricultural commodities. All products are sourced from verified farms and meet international quality standards.

Which countries do you export to?

We export to over 25 countries across Europe, North America, Asia, and the Middle East. Our major markets include the United Kingdom, United States, Germany, China, India, and various other countries. We have established logistics networks to ensure efficient delivery worldwide.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the product type. Generally, we require a minimum of one 20-foot container for most products. However, we can accommodate smaller orders for sample testing or trial shipments. Contact us for specific MOQ details for your desired product.

How do you ensure product quality?

Quality assurance is our top priority. We conduct rigorous inspections at every stage - from farm selection to final packaging. All products undergo laboratory testing for moisture content, purity, and contaminants. We hold HACCP and ISO 22000 certifications and provide detailed quality certificates with each shipment.

What documentation do you provide?

We provide complete export documentation including Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, Quality/Analysis Certificate, Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, and any other documentation required by your country's import regulations.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms including Letter of Credit (LC), Telegraphic Transfer (TT), and other internationally recognized payment methods. For new clients, we typically require advance payment or LC at sight. Terms can be negotiated based on order volume and business relationship.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping time varies by destination. Generally, sea freight to Europe takes 3-4 weeks, to North America 4-5 weeks, and to Asia 3-6 weeks depending on the specific port. Air freight is available for urgent orders with delivery within 5-7 days to most destinations.

Can you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for quality evaluation. Sample costs and shipping fees are typically borne by the buyer, but these costs can be deducted from your first order. Samples are usually sent via courier and arrive within 3-5 business days.
